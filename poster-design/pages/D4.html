Conclusion

Applications

Software like this, once fully-functional, has several useful applications. The obvious is for music information retrieval purposes, where it could be quite useful for aiding researches in annotating non-notated music.

Looking away from MIR techniques, the same sort of algorithm could be combined with speech-recognition software to automatically annotated panel discussions or podcasts, making them much more accessible for hearing-impaired users, as well as easily searchable.
